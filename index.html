About me

Born and raised in New York City, I grew up fascinated with film photography, travel, and boxing. These interests continue today. In my free time, I'm often trying to improve the quality of the pictures I take, learning from my older brother, training boxing and other martial arts in purusit of competiton, and travelling wherever I can. I often travel on my own to not only find opportunities for the aforementioned interests, taking photos of the places I encounter, or training at martial arts schools I come across along the way, but more importantly to meet, learn from, and befriend people from all over and expand my perspective of the world as a whole. These interests have definitely played a role in my outlook towards my work. I find protective equipment fascinating, even having had a brief opportunity to work on a wrestling helmet in the past, largely from my background and experience with combat sports. Seeing how different places around the world design solutions for their population based on environment, convenience, and standards has also stayed with me when thinking of solutions to problems I come across today. Learning from some of the people I've met across my travels has also helped me find clarity on what it is I want my output into the world to be. Approaching graduation, my goal as an engineer is to work on projects that really benefit the people using it, and wanting my work to contribute towards solutions that leave the world better off because of it.
